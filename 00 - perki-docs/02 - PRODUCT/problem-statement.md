# PROBLEM STATEMENT

People pay for dozens of subscriptions and services but use only a fraction of the perks they 
actually have access to. Value is lost because:
- Perks are hidden
- Perks are scattered across apps, emails, and portals
- People forget what they’re entitled to
- Providers don’t surface benefits proactively

Perki solves this by centralising everything into one clean, personalised interface.
