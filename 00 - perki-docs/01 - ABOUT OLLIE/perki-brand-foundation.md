# PERKI — BRAND FOUNDATION (Phase 1)
### The strategic core everything else calibrates to · Draft for sign-off · 2026-06-03

> Purpose: lock who Perki *is* before any palette, type, or logo work. UI UX Pro Max, design-system, and Brand Voice all calibrate to this.

## Brand essence (one paragraph)
Perki is the calm, trustworthy layer that surfaces the value you're already paying for. It turns the perks scattered across your subscriptions and memberships — forgotten, buried, unclaimed — into one clear, personal inventory, then quietly makes sure none of it goes to waste. It doesn't hunt for deals or shout about savings; it simply hands you what's already yours, with the precision of a good analyst and the ease of a great concierge. The result is a small, repeated moment of relief: *"I didn't know I had that."*

## Three adjectives
1. **Trustworthy** — fintech-grade, read-only, never touches your money. Calm and credible, not salesy.
2. **Effortless** — the work happens for you. Zero faff, one clean view, value surfaced without you digging.
3. **Rewarding** — every interaction can end in a quiet win — money or value you didn't know you had.

_Supporting tone: premium, precise, understated. Confident without hype._

## The feeling (what gold / purple / slate should evoke)
- **Gold (#F59E0B) — earned value & trust.** The "found money" moment; warmth and reward, but premium, not bargain-bin.
- **Purple (#8B5CF6) — the smart layer.** The intelligent tech quietly doing the matching and remembering in the background.
- **Slate (#0F172A) — calm & secure.** Serious, fintech-grade reassurance; the steady ground the gold sits on.

Emotional payoff in one line: **a quiet "oh, nice" — pleasant surprise, never a hard sell.**

## Positioning
- **One-line:** *The value you're already paying for — surfaced.*
- **What Perki is:** a personal, cross-provider perk inventory that makes hidden value visible and usable.
- **What Perki is NOT (anti-positioning):** not a coupon/deal-hunter (Honey, TopCashback) · not loud or gamified · not "another subscription to manage" · not a bank or a spender of your money.

## Audience anchor
Value-conscious but time-poor. Pays for plenty, uses a fraction, and would rather be *told* what they're owed than dig for it. Trust and ease beat discounts.

## Decisions this locks for the next phases
- Visual mood = **premium-calm with a reward warmth** (not playful, not austere).
- Voice = **clear, precise, quietly reassuring** — informs the Brand Voice pass.
- Gold is the hero/value colour; purple is the action/intelligence accent; slate is the calm base. (Consistent with the live design system.)

## Visual direction v1 (2026-06-03) — signed off on essence/adjectives/one-liner
Ollie's vision, refined together. **Warmer than the original dark-slate fintech route.**

**The mark — primary (reverse):** a deep indigo "coin" (squircle) holding a **gold P**, on an eggshell ground. Reads as a trusted vault surfacing the value (gold) inside. Subtle dimensional sheen + faint gold inner rings for a minted-coin feel; kept quiet so it scales.
- Variations: gold-coin/indigo-P alt · monochrome indigo outline · reverse for dark placements.
- The gold can extend into a faint coin-engraving (guilloché) texture field behind hero/deck sections.

**Palette (v1):**
| Role | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Eggshell | `#F4F0E6` | Primary surface / background |
| Indigo | `#2B2A6E` | Lead brand colour — the P, text, primary UI |
| Gold | `#E0A93B` | Hero / value accent |
| Deep gold | `#B07C1A` | Dimensional roll-off, borders |
| Ink | `#23202A` | Body text |

**Shift to note:** this replaces the live dark-slate website theme — moving to a light, eggshell-led system. Re-skin is a later phase task.

**Type:** geometric P pairs with a humanist-geometric sans; confirm pairing in Phase 2 (Pro Max). IBM Plex Sans remains a safe body candidate.
