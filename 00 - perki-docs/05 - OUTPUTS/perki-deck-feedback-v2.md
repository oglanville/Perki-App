## 0. The big one — kill the AI tells
Across the whole deck, three habits scream "AI wrote this." Fix them everywhere:
1. **Em dashes (—).** The single biggest tell. Replace with a full stop, a comma, or "and". The current deck is full of them. Every rewrite below avoids them.
2. **Rule-of-three triads** ("scattered across apps, emails and portals"; "calm, precise and reassuring"). Vary the rhythm. Use one concrete noun, not three abstract ones.
3. **Soft brand-words** ("surfaced", "seamless", "leverage", "quietly", "unlock"). Use plain verbs: shows, finds, pays, reminds.

**Recommended workflow to de-AI fast:** run the copy through **`brand-voice:enforce-voice`** (your installed Brand Voice skill). It applies the Perki voice guide and rewrites to tone. For the deck specifically, pair it with **`design:ux-copy`** (punchy microcopy) and **`design:design-critique`** (layout/hierarchy review). A simple manual pass: search the deck for "—" and delete every one; then read each line aloud and cut any word you wouldn't say to a friend.
