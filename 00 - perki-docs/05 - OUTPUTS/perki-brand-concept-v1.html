How it evolves into a full identity

Token system

The gold token flexes: app icon, favicon, loading state, and a “claimed” stamp on perks. One shape, many jobs.

Guilloché pattern

The fine texture on the token becomes a brand pattern — deck dividers, card backs, empty states — depth without weight.

Light theme retheme

Product shifts from dark slate to eggshell + indigo text + gold accents. A real but worthwhile re-tokening (the design-system phase handles it).

Logo variants

Primary lockup, mono indigo, reversed on indigo, and gold-foil on dark for premium moments (the pitch deck cover).
